Management Development Programme
Certificate programs offered by Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology & Cholamandalam MS Risk Services
Salient Features of the Training Programs
Industry-Academia Collaboration
- Jointly conducted by Chola MS Risk Services, a leader in risk engineering solutions, and RGIPT, a premier institute in energy and petroleum education.
- Blending practical industrial expertise with academic rigor.
Comprehensive Curriculum
Covers critical topics in:
- HAZOP (Hazard and Operability Study) Methodology
- Process Safety Management (PSM)
- Environmental Risk Assessment & Compliance
- Regulatory Frameworks (Indian & International)
Hands-on Training and Case Studies
- Real-world case studies from oil & gas, chemical, and process industries.
- Interactive HAZOP workshops using standard templates and tools.
- Exposure to quantitative and qualitative risk analysis techniques.
Expert Faculty & Trainers
- Sessions by seasoned professionals from Chola MS with field experience.
- Renowned academicians and researchers from RGIPT
Certification
- Joint Certification from Chola MS Risk Services and RGIPT upon successful completion.
- Valuable for professionals aiming for career advancement in EHS, safety engineering, and process industries.
Flexible Delivery Modes
- Hybrid Format: Choose between physical (on-campus) and virtual (online live) participation.
- Customized for working professionals and industry participants.
Target Audience
- Process Engineers, HSE Officers, Plant Managers, Compliance Officers, and Regulatory Professionals.
- Suitable for Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Chemical, Fertilizer, Pharma, and Energy sectors.
Networking Opportunity
- Platform to interact with industry leaders, fellow professionals, and subject matter experts.
- Opportunity to discuss real-time challenges and innovative solutions in process safety and environmental management.
