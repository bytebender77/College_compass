RIPE 2025
Summer Research Internship in Petroleum and Energy
for
Students Pursuing 2nd and 3rd year of B Tech/1st year MSc Programme
Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais 229 304 (www.rgipt.ac.in)
Applications are invited from bonafied B Tech/M Sc students of prominent institutions of India for the Summer Research Internship in Petroleum and Energy domain of Six Weeks duration (May 19, 2025 to July 11, 2025). Students must complete all examinations of the academic session 2024-2025 before the commencement of the Internship. Students, who will be completing 1st year M Sc or 2nd year to the pre-final year of their B Tech programme in 2024-2025, shall only be eligible. Selection will be based on academic achievements. Accommodation in the Hostel and Mess Facility (Chargeable basis) shall be provided to all the selected candidates during the course of this programme. Students will be provided a certificate on successful completion of the programme. There is no Fee for this internship programme.
