RGIPT Scholarship for B Tech and IDD
Eligibility
- Students whose annual family income is ≤ Rs. 5lakhs are eligible to apply for this same scholarship.
- Amongst the eligible candidates, latest CPI/SPI (in 1st Semester) will be considered as the criteria for the selection.
- Candidate once selected will continue to receive fee waiver in subsequent semesters subjected to the following conditions:
- His/her CPI/SPI does not fall below 6.5 and he/she does not receive any Fail grade (F, I, Z).
- The candidate needs to submit family income certificate each year and the income shall not exceed the threshold limit (Of Rs. 5 lakhs).
- Once the Scholarship discontinued, the candidate will not be eligible for reconsideration.
- Any vacancy created thereof shall be filled with the next candidate in the waitlist, which was prepared at the time of deciding the award.
Benefit
- Under McM Scholarship, 10% students of each discipline (rounded off to the next higher number) will receive 25% waiver in the tuition.
Documents
- Income Certificate
- Last Semester Grade-sheet
Central Scholarship for B Tech and IDD Students
The Scheme of Top Class Education for SC students has been selected for implemented through NSP.
Eligibility
To be eligible, an applicant must–
- Belong to the Scheduled Caste category
- Have completed Class 12
- Have taken admission in a full-time course in a notified institution through general selection criteria prescribed by respective institutions
- Have an annual income of less than INR 6 lakh from all the sources
Benefit
The selected students will receive –
- Full tuition fee and non-refundable charges of up to INR 2.00 lakh per annum for private sector institutions and up to INR 3.72 lakh per annum for private sector flying clubs for Commercial Pilot Training and Type Rating Courses
- Living expense: INR 2,220 per month
- Books and stationery: INR 3,000 per annum
- Computer/Laptop with UPS and Printer of any reputed brand: INR 45,000 (one-time assistance)
Documents
- Caste Certificate
- Income Certificate
- Bank account details of the institution and the student
- Proof of admission
- Scorecard of entrance exam qualified (if applicable)
- Fee structure of the program

State Scholarship for B Tech and IDD Students of RGIPT
Eligibility
To be eligible, an applicant must –
- The applicant must be a resident of Madhya Pradesh.
- He/ She must have passed Class 12 with 70% or more marks (if appeared through Secondary Education Board of Madhya Pradesh) or have obtained 85% or more marks (if appeared through CBSE/ICSE Board).
- The applicant must have an annual parent/guardian income of less than INR 6 lakh from all sources.
- The applicant must have enrolled at an undergraduate level of course at a recognized university or institution.
- Engineering Aspirants must have qualified JEE Main exam with a rank less than 1, 50,000.
Benefit
- The students enrolling in an engineering program at a government/non-government college will receive full fees for the course. However, the students enrolling in a private college will receive INR 1.5 Lakh or actual course fee (whichever is less).
Documents
The candidates while filling up the application form must submit the following documents:-
- Aadhaar Card of Candidate
- Annual Income Certificate
- Domicile of Madhya Pradesh
- Class 12th Marksheet
- Proof of Admission
- 10th Scorecard and Certificates
- Photo of Applicant
- Complete Bank Details like Name of Bank, IFSC or MICR Code, Branch Name, etc.
- Contact Number
