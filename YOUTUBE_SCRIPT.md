### [12:00 - 13:00] How It Works - Technical Overview

**[Screen: Show architecture diagram or code]**

> "Let me quickly explain how this works under the hood:
>
> 1. When you ask a question, it's sent to the FastAPI backend
> 2. The backend uses FAISS to search for similar content in the vector store
> 3. It retrieves the top 5 most relevant document chunks
> 4. These chunks are sent to the Hugging Face LLM with your question
> 5. The LLM generates an answer based on the retrieved context
> 6. The answer and sources are returned to the frontend
>
> This is the RAG pattern - Retrieval Augmented Generation. It combines the power of vector search with language models to provide accurate, source-backed answers."
